Rules and Regulations

How many people can be there in a team? ▼
Each team can have up to 4 members.
Can a team consist of participants from different universities? ▼
Yes, teams can consist of members from different universities.
Who can participate in the Robotic Car Battle? ▼
Anyone with an interest in robotics can participate.
Do the participants need to attend physically? ▼
No, the event can be attended online.
What kind of robots can the participants build? ▼
Participants can build any battle-ready robot within the provided specifications.
Will I get a certificate of participation? ▼
Yes, all participants will receive a certificate of participation.
Does the competition require prior robotics experience? ▼
No prior experience is necessary, but it’s beneficial.
How can I register for the Robotic Car Battle? ▼
You can register on the official Robotic Car Battle website.
